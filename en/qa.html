Frequently Asked Questions

A: The "Autumn Gose Walk Rally" will be held on Sunday, September 21, 2025, from 8:30 AM to 4:00 PM. The event will proceed in light rain, but in case of severe weather, it will be postponed to Sunday, October 19, 2025.

A: For reservations and inquiries, please visit → Reservation & Contact

A: No, parking is not available. Since other events will also be taking place nearby on the same day, we recommend using public transportation as much as possible. If you are driving, please use one of the nearby paid parking lots.

A: Participation is free, but using the shuttle bus requires a fee of 200 JPY/day.

A: This is a photo rally-style walk rally where participants explore the attractions of Gose city on foot. Using a digital map, participants visit as many checkpoints as possible within the time limit, take photos, and collect points, enjoying a game-like experience while discovering Gose's hidden charms.

A: Please bring a smartphone or digital camera that can take photos and display them immediately. A smartphone is ideal as you will use a digital map.

A: Primarily for families in their 30s–40s, or those interested in relocating or living in two locations. Residents of Gose who want to learn more about their city are also welcome. Participation is open to anyone.

A: The event is divided into three main areas:

  • Gose Town Area: Near JR Gose Station, suitable for beginners and families.
  • Koya Kaido Area: Explore scenic spots and discover Gose’s attractions.
  • Myth Area: For experienced walkers, enjoy breathtaking nature and legendary sites.

A: No, registration for each course is not required. You are free to choose and walk any course you like.

A: The schedule is as follows:

  • Assembly & Reception: 8:30 AM – 9:30 AM
  • Start: 9:00 AM – 10:00 AM
    ※ Multiple start times to avoid congestion.
  • Checkpoint Visit: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM
  • Goal: 1:30 PM – 3:00 PM
  • Point Tally & Lottery: 3:00 PM – 3:30 PM
  • performance of Susuki lanterns and taiko drumming3:30 PM – 4:00 PM
  • End of the event: 4:00 PM～

A: The assembly and start location is JR Gose Station Square. The goal location is Katsuragi Park. At Katsuragi Park, the goal location, the afternoon will feature company booths, a consultation corner on relocation and settlement, and fun activities for children such as a candy grab.

A: A shuttle bus will operate within specific areas (paid). Maximum 9 passengers per bus, wait times may occur. With a 200 JPY cooperation fee and showing your wristband, unlimited rides are allowed. The last bus departs at 2:00 PM.

A: Not mandatory, but recommended as the event is limited to 200 participants (first-come, first-served). Applications are accepted via Google Form. Notifications for rain cancellations will be sent to those who reserve in advance.

A: Based on points earned, participants can enter a lottery to win accommodations (1 night with 2 meals), common vouchers usable at participating stores, or vouchers for hot spring facilities (Gose Takara-yu, Kamokimi-no-Yu, etc.).

A: The digital map shows restaurants, toilets, and convenience stores and supermarkets will be listed as water supply points. At the goal location, Katsuragi Park, there will be sweets for children. Note that eating in the central park area is restricted.

A: Please note the following:

  • Participation is limited to 200 people (first-come, first-served). Exceeding the limit may result in denial of participation.
  • If you are unwell on the day, please refrain from participating.
  • The organizers may change some content without prior notice.
  • If bringing pets, please notify the staff.

A: At the goal location, Katsuragi Park, company booths and relocation consultation corners will be available to answer questions about local jobs and provide relocation information. Feel free to consult them.
